Education

I previously attened Eastern Illinois University. I graduated in 2016 with a bachelor's degree in 2D Art. While there, I was in various organization. I was a member of Alpha Sigma Tau sorority. I was also a member of Art Associtaion, Women's Empowerment League and EIU Striders (a running club.)

I just recently returned from a semester abroad in Ireland. There, I studied humanities at Carlow College St. Patrick's.

I am currently studying Graphic Design at Parkland College. It has been quite an adjustment going from painting and drawing classes to working mainly with computers. It's a lot less of a messy process, though!

Some of my favorite mediums to work with are:

  • Oil paint
  • Water colors
  • Acrylic paint
  • Digital platforms
  • Pen and ink
  • Colored pencil

    Home Life

    I currently live around the Champaign area with my two dogs, Boz and Yogi. Boz is a two-year-old yellow lab. Yogi is a one-year-old aussie-poo (Austrailian shepherd/poodle mix) Both are handful!

    I grew up on my family's farm with lots of black angus cattle. In addition to cattle, we also grow corn and beans.

    I like to go to the downtown areas of Champaign/Urbana. I usally take my dogs for a walk through the parks where they can go out and get plenty of attention from other people!

    Personal Interests

    During my free time, I have many hobbies and acitivies I like to spend time doing. I enjoy long distance running and weight lifting out. I also like spending time outside, whether it's going for a hike or simply relaxing outside in the sun.

    I also like to spend time drawing, painting and making different pieces of art. I just discover how to make digital painting, so I have been spending a lot of my time trying to practice more at it.

    I enjoy traveling and planning. Whever there is an opportunity to travel, I try to take advantage of it. There are plenty of places I enjoy returning to like Florida or North Carolina, but I alway like discovering new places!
